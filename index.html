Professional by Practice

FACES Consulting operates as a student-led consulting firm at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign that holds itself to a professional standard in client work. We partner with startups, entrepreneurs, and small to mid-sized companies across industries, delivering clear analysis and practical recommendations in strategy, financial analysis, marketing, and data analytics. Our teams are structured, accountable, and expected to meet defined expectations for preparation, communication, and execution. With an active client presence in Chicago and Champaign-Urbana, FACES approaches each engagement with discipline, follow-through, and respect for the responsibility of advising real businesses.
